Fried Rice

Description

Fried rice is a dish of cooked rice that has been stir-fried in a wok or a frying pan and is usually mixed with other ingredients such as eggs, vegetables, seafood, or meat. It is often eaten by itself or as an accompaniment to another dish.

Ingredients

  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Sesame Oil
  • Soy Sauce
  • Rice

Steps on how to cook

  1. Heat wok until it is very hot. Add butter in a wok
  2. Add onion and garlic and fry for a minute
  3. Add whisked eggs in the wok and scramble them
  4. Mix scrambled eggs and rice
  5. Add cooked rice, soy sauce, sesame oil, and toss well
